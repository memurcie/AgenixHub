# Enterprise AI Copilot

**Category:** Enterprise AI Applications

## Definition

An Enterprise AI Copilot is a secure, task-oriented AI system designed to assist employees using internal enterprise data, workflows, and permissions.

## Key Characteristics

- **Integration with enterprise systems:** Connects to ERP, CRM, HR, document management systems
- **Role-based access control (RBAC):** Users only see data they're authorized to access
- **Audit logging and compliance:** Tracks all queries, responses, and data access
- **Custom knowledge base integration:** Leverages proprietary documents and databases
- **Contextual awareness:** Understands business processes, organizational structure, and user roles
